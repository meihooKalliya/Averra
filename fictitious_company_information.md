## Cost & ROI Analysis

The most expensive thing in your sales process isn't your CRM, your stack, or your office. It's the hours between a lead going cold and a human picking up the phone.

Averra removes that gap entirely. Every conversation your team would've had — the follow-ups, the cold opens, the qualification calls — happens at a fraction of what it costs to staff for them. Not because quality is cut. Because the overhead is.

The teams using Averra aren't just moving faster. They're operating at a cost structure their competitors genuinely can't reverse-engineer with headcount alone.
